Preserving Heritage Through Technology

ArchaeoKZ was developed to bridge the gap between ancient history and modern technology. Our goal is to digitize the rich archaeological heritage of Kazakhstan, making Bronze Age artifacts accessible to researchers, students, and enthusiasts worldwide. Through an open-access catalog and AI-driven identification tools, we aim to preserve the past for future generations.
